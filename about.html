Hi, I'm Jeremiah Pitts.

I am an aerospace and mechanical engineering student studying programming. I love adventure and the park near my house is my “second home”. Whenever I’m not in class, I’m usually having fun biking through trail systems; although not a fanatic for nature, I love the open doors, cooking, chess, math, engineering, robotics, and coding. Having a strong knack for adventure, I’ve found myself pursuing the deep forests of ingenuity and the shallow marshes of integrated mathematics. I have a true passion and a seemingly unbreakable focus when it comes to achieving, am rarely listless, and I love coming across new problems only to fix them, although I would be more satisfied in the absence of error. Pushing myself has led to drastic growth and improvement in many areas, through both my weaknesses and successes. Please enjoy the rest of my website.
